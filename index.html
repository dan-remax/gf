      Choose Your Path

      Franchise opportunities vary by market. Depending on availability, qualified candidates may be considered for one of three paths.

      1

      Lead a Market

      Master Franchise Partner

      For qualified leaders with the vision, capital and operating experience to help grow the REMAX brand across an approved country or territory. As a Master Franchise Partner, you may have the opportunity to develop a national network, support office expansion and help bring the REMAX model to more agents, brokers and communities.

      Best for: Experienced entrepreneurs, investors and operators ready to build at scale.

      2

      Convert What You’ve Built

      Existing Real Estate Business Conversion

      Already leading a real estate company? In select markets, qualified owners may be able to bring an existing business into the REMAX network. This path allows established operators to grow from their current foundation while gaining the visibility, systems and support of a global real estate brand.

      Best for: Brokerage owners and real estate leaders ready to grow from an established base.

      3

      Open Something New

      New Office Franchise

      In select markets, qualified candidates may be considered for the opportunity to open a new REMAX office. This path is designed for entrepreneurial leaders who want to launch with brand recognition, business systems and network support from day one.

      Best for: Operators ready to recruit, lead and grow a new office in an approved market.

      Bring the Balloon to a New Market

      In approved countries where REMAX does not currently have a presence, qualified candidates may be considered for country-level expansion, business conversion or office franchise opportunities.

      These are select markets where the right local leader can help introduce the REMAX brand, build relationships and create the foundation for long-term network growth.

      Available paths may include:

      Master Franchise Partner
      Lead country-level growth by developing the REMAX brand, supporting franchise expansion and helping build a network of offices and agents across an approved market.

      Convert an Existing Real Estate Business
      Bring an established real estate operation into the REMAX network and continue growing from an existing foundation.

      Launch a New Office Franchise
      Start-up office opportunities may be reviewed on a case-by-case basis in select approved markets.

      Grow Where the Brand Is Already Moving

      In countries and territories where REMAX already has a presence, opportunities may still exist for qualified candidates to grow through office ownership, business conversion or agent affiliation.

      These markets already have REMAX representation. The opportunity is to build within it.

      Available paths may include:

      Convert an Existing Real Estate Business
      Transition an established brokerage under the REMAX brand, where available, and continue growing with global brand recognition and network support.

      Launch a New Office Franchise
      New office franchise opportunities may be considered in select territories, subject to availability, qualification and approval.

      Join as an Agent
      Real estate professionals interested in joining an existing REMAX office can explore agent opportunities through the REMAX network.
